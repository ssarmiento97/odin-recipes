Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Everyone needs to know how to make a classic grilled cheese sandwich. Whether you're a beginner cook or an old pro, you'll come back to this top-rated grilled cheese recipe again and again.

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Sliced cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet.
  3. Add 1 slice of cheese. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  4. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
